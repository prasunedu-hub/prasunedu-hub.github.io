About MSA

MARSHA SPORTS ARCADE (MSA) is a premier tennis academy in Kolkata dedicated to nurturing young talent and professional athletes. Our structured training programs, certified coaches, and competitive exposure help players reach their highest potential.

Tennis is more than just a game. It teaches us focus, fitness, patience, perseverance, and discipline. This academy will serve as a platform where aspiring players can learn professional skills, develop confidence, become sensitive human being and chase their dreams both on and off the court.

MARSHA SPORTS ARCADE (M.S.A.) is born to nurture quality tennis conditioning to churn out champions with nerves of steel and hearts of gold.
Our ITF-standard blue synthetic courts are crafted for professional training and competitive excellence. Precision court markings, tournament-grade nets, and high-safety perimeter fencing ensure a world-class playing environment.
